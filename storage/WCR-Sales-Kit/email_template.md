# Cold Email Templates — South Jersey Trades Outreach

> **Framework:** Hormozi Hook Framework + PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
> Each email leads with a specific hook type, agitates the cost of inaction, and presents a low-friction CTA.
> **Volume target:** 100-200/day with proper warmup and infrastructure.
> **Follow-up:** 5-7 times. Most responses come on follow-ups 3-5.

---

## Quick Reference: Personalization Checklist

Before sending any email, replace these placeholders:

| Placeholder | Example |
|---|---|
| `{{Rep Name}}` | Sales rep or agent name |
| `{{Business Name}}` | Carl D'Angelo Plumbing |
| `{{First Name}}` | Carl |
| `{{City}}` | Collingswood |
| `{{Trade Type}}` | Plumber / Plumbing |
| `{{Preview URL}}` | raw.githack.com link |
| `{{Your Phone}}` | Your phone number |
| `{{Your Email}}` | Your email address |

**Best send times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 7:00–8:30 AM or 5:00–6:30 PM (before/after their workday)

**Key rule:** Personalize the opener. Reference something specific — their Google reviews, their service area, a recent job listing. Generic emails get deleted. Specific emails get read.
