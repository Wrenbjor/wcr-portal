Starter includes 1 round of revisions. Professional includes 2. Premium includes ongoing monthly updates. Additional one-off changes are billed at $50/hour.

**Is there a contract?**
No long-term contracts. Monthly plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

**Can I upgrade later?**
Absolutely. You can move from Starter to Professional or Premium at any time. We'll credit your original setup fee toward the upgrade.
