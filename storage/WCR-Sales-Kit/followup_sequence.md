# Follow-Up Email Sequence

> **Framework:** Hormozi Lead Nurture System
> Speed to lead matters — contact within 5 minutes of first interaction.
> Multi-channel: email + text + call. Never send "just checking in."
> Every touchpoint delivers value. Assume they didn't see the last one.
> Most responses come on follow-ups 3-5. Don't quit early.

---

## Multi-Channel Notes

- **Day 0:** Email + text the preview link
- **Day 2:** Email only
- **Day 5:** Email + call (leave voicemail referencing the social proof email)
- **Day 7:** Email only
- **Day 10:** Call + email the value stack
- **Day 12:** Text the urgency message
- **Day 14:** Email only (breakup)

**Track everything:** Know your cost per lead, responses per email, and which follow-up number converts most. Double down on what works.
