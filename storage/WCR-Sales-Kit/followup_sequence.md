> **Usage:** Send Email 1 (your initial outreach/cold call follow-up) on Day 1. This sequence covers Days 3, 7, and 14.
> Replace all `{{placeholders}}` before sending.

## Notes for Personalization

- **{{first_name}}** — use the owner's first name if known; otherwise use "there"
- **[trade]** — match their specific trade (e.g., "plumbing", "handyman", "towing")
- **[city]** — use their actual city (e.g., "Maple Shade", "Cherry Hill", "Camden")
- **Review count tip** — if they have 50+ Google reviews, mention it in Email 3 to build credibility
- **24-hour businesses** — for businesses marked as 24/7, emphasize the "customers find you at 2am" angle in any of these emails
